The schools of magic refer to how spells are classified for the sake of study. However, the number, name, and function of the schools is hotly debated among scholars. Therefore, I have attempted to create a generalized set of schools which also more or less follows the those set forth by the Mage's guild in `Wilheim`.

## List of Schools

## Aether
This is the school of spirits and the non-corporeal. It allows you to summon spirits and is the school which most breaks from the standard of Ordered magic. 
*Common Damage Types:*
- radiant
- umbral
- force

## Divination
At its simplest, divination is the extension of the senses, both those of the body and those of the spirit. Divinations spells may allow you to see the flow of Mana or hear the thoughts of others. Some spells can even allow the user to sense the threads of fate, and the most powerful of these practitioners are even said to be able to directly affect the threads.

## Evocation
Evocation magic is the movement and empowerment of the physical world. This is the magic most tied to the physical world and tends to be flashier, with masters being able to infernos from a single spark or flood a room with a cup of water.
*Common Damage Types:*
- Fire
- Cold
- Slashing / Piercing / Bludgeoning

## Illusion
This is the magic of falsehoods, both of the senses and of the mind. The falsehoods this type of spell can create can be so intense that they actually kill the target, their mind unable to handle the dissonance between what they are seeing and what is actually occurring. The most infamous ability of this school is the ability of certain spells to affect a person's behavior with the most powerful of these spells being said to even be able to directly manipulate the memories of a target.
*Common Damage Types*:
- Psychic
## Transmutation
Transmutation is the temporary changing of properties of the natural world, including living beings. While most associate this school with parlor tricks, turning lead to gold, it is in reality one of the most flexible and potentially terrifying type of magic;  growing gills to breath underwater, stitching wounds, imparting rot, creating artificial life. 
*Common Damage Types:*
- Necrotic
- Shock
- Cold
