Mana is an innate part of every living thing. It is the energy of cycles with Life and Death being the cycle that most greatly creates Mana.
A living thing's mana connects its soul to the world around it. This is what allows for spells to be cast, the user shaping their mana to affect themselves or the world around them. However, this connection goes both ways. If a person exists near or exposed to a strong source of magic, that energy will flow back into the living thing. This results in the many of the Uncommon Ancestries and Sorcerers


# Types of Magic
Scholars have roughly defined three types of magic in the word: Ancient, Aether, and Ordered. Although many argue that Ancient and Miracle should be one category or that Miracles should be a subtype of Ancient.

## Arcane
Spell sigils, magic words, and rituals
Creating rituals and sigils is dangerous work and often destructive so a lot of research focuses on modifying existing spells or researching forgotten spells

## Miracles
Spells granted by powerful non-mortal beings

## Wild Magic
Everything else, the primal magic, magic that just happens and evades understanding
